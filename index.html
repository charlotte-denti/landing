What is Icare?

Your new favorite bracelet

We decided to develop a bracelet that, in response to a certain amount of UV, will become darker and darker, in order to warn of the risks of overexposure. .

The bracelet mixes the aesthetic of a normal bracelet with the utility of the capsule that you must watch to see the color changing to be aware of the exposure time to prevent your skin from sunburn. Moreover we have decided to increase the range of our product with a sportswear model, a basic model basically for adults and one model for children.
